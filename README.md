# World Cup 2026 Viewing Handbook · 观赛手册

A mobile-first prediction playground for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, Bay Area edition.

- **赛程** — all 52 US group-stage matches with live win/draw/win probability bars from [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com) prediction markets
- **小组** — tap teams to rank each group (top 2 advance, 3rd goes to best-third pool)
- **对阵** — interactive knockout bracket with auto-carry of your picks, plus a live champion-odds board
- **分享** — generate a shareable prediction card image

Predictions are saved in your browser's localStorage — everyone who opens the link gets their own copy to play with.

Static site, no build step: [Preact](https://preactjs.com) + [HTM](https://github.com/developit/htm) inlined, odds fetched client-side from the public Polymarket Gamma API.

For entertainment only. Kickoff times: check the official FIFA app.
